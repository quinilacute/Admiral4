Tracking

Technology plays a major role at Admiral Trucker (Nig) Ltd., from equipment monitoring/tracking to information systems to customer
service. Our goal is to drive costs down and efficiency levels up.

In conjuction with Globacomm Telecoms (Glo), our entire fleet is equipped with communication technology. This gives us instant two-
way communications with our drivers for dispatching and directions.

Working with maps and communication devices, our drivers can request directions from their current position to the next stop on
their route, or in the unlikely event of a breakdown, to the nearest service facility.

We moni tor the engine data constantly to help our service departments keep our fleet in top performing condition. GPS information
from our tractors allow us to track your loads 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
